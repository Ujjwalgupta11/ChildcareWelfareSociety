What we do?

Child Care Welfare Society in Agra provides free education for underprivileged children in various slums for 19 years. Operating in locations like Nagla Budi, Budi ki Nagla, Adarsh Nagar, Jail Road, and Azaad Nagar, we offer classes from Nursery to Class X after regular school hours, currently supporting 180 students. Our holistic approach includes medical camps, training programs, health education, cancer awareness, sports events, festivals, and more. We aim to nurture the overall well-being of children and women, fostering a brighter and empowered community.

About Our Charity

It is a registered Non-Governmental organization (NGO) presently working in the city of Taj founded in 2004, by Mrs. Poonam Lahoti and Mrs. Neena Singhal. Our mission is to locate children from dark alleys, shabby and backward areas still suffering from poverty, ill-health and illiteracy and provide them their basic need and right to education, good health and better lives which would help them in becoming a better and a responsible citizen of our nation We started with mere 2 founding members, but with time and perseverance, today we have 20 members, including 4 medical doctors. In addition to that, we have managed to build up a competent staff of 20 teachers who manage an approximate number of 450 students. All these students are provided education from class Nursery to class X in 6 centers from 3:00 - 5:30. Since our schools are run in rented space of schools, space is available only after the regular school is over. Not only for schooling education, but we have also provided provisions for medical camps (dental, eye, medicine, Gynae), training programs (tailoring, embroidery, envelop making, beautician), health education, cancer awareness programs, Sports days, festivals etc.
